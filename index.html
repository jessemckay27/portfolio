About Me

Hello! My name is Jesse McKay. I am 34 years old, from Portland, OR. I have two children, Natalie (age 8) and Alexis (age 9). My interest in programming and computers started when my parents bought our first computer in 1995. It was a Gateway, which showed up at our front door in a big cardboard box covered with black and white cow markings. From that day onward I have always had an interest in computers. My love for gaming eventually steered me towards an interest in assembling computers, which lead to an interest in coding. After learning some basic skills at Code Academy, I decided to sign up to learn at Epicodus.
